Benefits

Precision Farming: Farmers can optimize resource allocation by receiving accurate predictions, allowing for better planning of irrigation, fertilization, and pest control.

Risk Management: Predictions help farmers anticipate potential yield fluctuations due to weather conditions, enabling them to implement risk mitigation strategies.

Cost Reduction: By optimizing resource usage, farmers can reduce unnecessary expenses on inputs like water and fertilizer.

Increased Productivity: With more informed decision-making, farmers can enhance overall productivity and crop yield.

Timely Interventions: Early detection of issues such as pest infestations or nutrient deficiencies allows for timely interventions, preventing potential yield losses.

Adaptability: The model can adapt to changing conditions, providing dynamic and responsive predictions throughout the growing season.
